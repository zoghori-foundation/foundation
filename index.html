our approach

In our socities today, there are a lot of challenges that need you and I, that need solutions, solutions that can be long-lasting, life-changing and refreshing. Among them is the challenge of marginalisation and inequitable sharing and distribution of county and national resources. This brings with it a lot of disparities such as high poverty levels, lack of proper access to quality education hence higher dependency ratios. That's why, Zoghori Sacco Society Limited through Zoghori Foundation and it's partners - The Zoghori Champions of Hope, has resolved to help in building and transforming the society through the mobilisation of both financial and human resources. Ours is a calling anchored on the foundations of Building & Transformation of people's lives through activities such as offering sustainable education scholarships, mentorship programs, counselling services to our youths and supporting environmental conservation initiatives.

We believe in BUILDING & TRANSFORMATION of one another through concerted and unified efforts, planning and prudential management of acquired resources to achieve our mantra of TRANSFORM A LIFE TO CHANGE THE WORLD

QUALIFYING FOR THE SCHOLARSHIP

For order, openness, efficiency and accountability, the foundation through its able leaderdhip, has set aside afew parameters for any individual to be accepted for sponsorship through Zoghori Foundation.

THE CONDITIONS ARE:

One must:
Be a needy child.
Have excelled well in their K.C.P.E exams,
Earned a position in a government secondary school in Kenya.
Be able to verify the position of need.

These conditions are applicable to all, without bias!

ZOGHORI CHAMPION OF HOPE

Zoghori Champion of Hope

This is an individual or institution who is willing to commit a certain amount of money, either given monthly, quarterly, annually to the Zoghori Foundation
in ensuring a needy situation will have a solution.

Our belief is that there is hope in every young person to succeed in life no matter his or her background,
hence our responsibility to partner with Zoghori Champions of Hope to fulfil their dreams.

Qualifying for Zoghori Champion of Hope

By either the individual or institution making a commitment to commit a monthly donation of kshs.50 to Kshs.1,000 and above, paid either monthly ,quarterly, half yearly and yearly.

By seeking a donation from kshs.50 is that no one with a heart to touch a needy situation is locked out.

  Our History

Zoghori Foundation is the vehicle for Zoghori Sacco Society Ltd for Corporate Social Responsibility.

The current Zoghori Foundation's flagship project of the Foundation is an Education SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME for bright but less fortunate children
who are facing difficulties in paying school fees for both secondary and university education.

Zoghori Foundation was established in 2019 as a registered trust in recognition of the
need to enhance the existing Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives by the society

At that point, the society felt it was its duty to not only grow
financially but also develop a deeper commitment to help enhance the growth of the community

We believe that our role goes beyond profit-making to include promoting the long-term economic
prosperity and quality of life for everyone in our communities

If our people prosper, we prosper as a society and soar to unimaginable heights of achieving our motto
of BUILDING ONE ANOTHER

In the year 2016, Zoghori Sacco Society Limited successfully rolled out an Education program to pay
school fees for needy secondary school students as an integral part of its CSR projects

The program will continue in the days ahead, to enable these children to achieve their full potential
